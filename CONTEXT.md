# flatmate.ch Context

## 💡 Rule of Context
If it's not in this file, the AI doesn't know it.

## 🚀 Project Goal
Digitalize the shared flat (WG) search in the Swiss market by shifting the focus from the physical appearance of the flat to the people living in it. The core problem being solved is that for WGs, personal chemistry and personality fit are far more important than the property itself. The platform serves both:
- **Searcher Perspective:** Finding a WG where they truly fit in based on personality and lifestyle.
- **WG Perspective:** Finding a new flatmate that naturally matches the existing group dynamics.
